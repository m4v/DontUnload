Plugin for prevent vessels with parachutes from disappearing in atmosphere when they get 2.5km away.

Requirements
------------

KSP version 0.21.*

Installation
------------

Standard plugin installation. Just copy into your KSP's GameData directory.

Usage
-----

Vessels that are flying (vessels that are in atmosphere) and are controllable or have parachutes
will not be unloaded and put on rails (which usually leads to its destruction). Vessels
that go suborbital or land and aren't the active will be unloaded (if possible).
